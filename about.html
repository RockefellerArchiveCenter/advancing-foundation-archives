Some of us work at archival repositories, others are records management or archives consultants, still more are on staff at foundations and nonprofits.

At the 2019 Advancing Foundation Archives (AFA) meeting, we gathered for the first time in almost thirty years to talk about the challenges we all faced. That conversation was only the beginning. We built this online community to be an ongoing space for conversation and collaboration.
